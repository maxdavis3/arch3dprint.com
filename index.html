STUDIO SERVICES

Architectural Print Studio

01

Concept & Competition Models

Fast-turnaround competition models and design development studies. White nylon or full-color resin from Revit and ArchiCAD files. Physical form exploration to understand spatial quality before committing to detailed design.

02

Client Presentation Models

High-quality presentation models for planning submissions, developer pitches, and public consultations. Full-color, site-context models with surrounding buildings, landscape, and traffic infrastructure.

03

Construction-Scale Printing

Robotic concrete extrusion for bespoke structural wall elements, landscape features, and custom architectural components. Curvilinear and free-form geometry at building scale — without formwork cost.
